# Marketing Strategy

*How Verispect creates demand and captures it. Compliance-led, credibility-first, founder-voiced. Budget is mostly time, not cash.*

---

## Strategic premise
The buyer of a *compliance* product buys **trust before features**. So marketing's job #1 is to make Verispect (and Ajay) the **most credible, most honest voice on AI behavioural assurance** — then convert that credibility into snapshots → demos → revenue. We win on authority and proof, not ad spend.

## Three audiences, three messages (recap from positioning)
- **Engineers/CTOs** → "one line, zero latency, we never see your data, here's the code."
- **AI/compliance leads** → "article-by-article evidence, automated, for the 2026 deadline."
- **DPOs/legal** → "reduces your data risk; here's the DPA."

## Demand engines (in priority)

### 1. Founder-led content + SEO (compounding flagship)
Own the searches and the conversation around "EU AI Act monitoring," "LLM bias detection," "AI model drift," "post-market monitoring AI Act."
- **Cornerstone guides** (rank + lead magnet):
  - "EU AI Act Post-Market Monitoring (Art. 72): A Practical Guide for AI Teams"
  - "How to Detect LLM Drift in Production (with code)"
  - "Bias Testing for AI Hiring Tools Under the EU AI Act"
  - "Free DPIA Template for LLM Decision Systems"
- **Findings content** (PR-able, link magnets): "We probed {model} 1,000× for hiring bias — results."
- **Methodology transparency**: how our scoring works, its limits (builds trust precisely because it's honest).
- **Cadence:** 1 cornerstone/2 weeks + 3–4 LinkedIn posts/week (see sales/02).

### 2. Free tools / lead magnets (top-of-funnel capture)
- **Free Drift/Bias Snapshot** — one line, 5 min, returns a real 2-page report. The hero magnet.
- **Free DPIA template** (Word/PDF) — captures compliance-lead emails.
- **EU AI Act readiness checklist** — interactive or PDF.
- **"Is your model high-risk?" quiz** — 6 questions → classification + next steps + email capture.
Each magnet → email nurture (file 04) → demo.

### 3. Launch moments (spikes)
Product Hunt, Show HN, LinkedIn launch, relevant subreddits/communities, AI Act newsletters. See `02-launch-plan.md`.

### 4. Community & partnerships
- Be present/useful in Responsible AI, MLOps, AI Act LinkedIn/Slack/Discord communities.
- Partner with **RAI consultancies, boutique law firms, GRC platforms, fractional DPOs** → referral + channel for Enterprise.
- Guest on AI/compliance podcasts and newsletters (the origin story is great audio).

### 5. Paid (small, surgical, later)
Once messaging is proven organically: LinkedIn ads targeting Head of AI / DPO at high-risk-domain companies in EU/SG/UAE; retargeting site visitors with the sample report. Keep spend small (€500/mo) until CAC is proven. All claims pass the guardrails doc.

## Brand & voice
- **Voice:** technical peer + plain-spoken about the law. Credible, calm, slightly contrarian ("logging isn't monitoring").
- **Visual:** the existing purple (#7c6eff) + dark, clean, technical. Reports and site share one identity.
- **Tone law:** honest to a fault. We are allowed to scare people with *true* facts, never false ones (see guardrails).

## Marketing funnel KPIs
- Top: organic sessions, magnet downloads, snapshots run, LinkedIn followers/engagement.
- Mid: snapshot→demo rate, nurture email CTR, content→signup.
- Bottom: demos booked, demo→paid, CAC, attributed MRR.
- North star alignment: every campaign should drive **reports generated**.

## 90-day marketing sprint
- **Weeks 1–4:** launch site + free snapshot + 3 cornerstone posts; start LinkedIn cadence; build email nurture.
- **Weeks 5–8:** Product Hunt/HN launch; publish first findings piece; first 2 partner convos; DPIA + checklist magnets live.
- **Weeks 9–13:** first case study; podcast/newsletter guesting; turn on small LinkedIn retargeting; double down on the best-performing content.
