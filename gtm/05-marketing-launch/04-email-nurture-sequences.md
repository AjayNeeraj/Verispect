## Ongoing newsletter (all opted-in leads)
Monthly. Mix: one regulatory update (AI Act timeline/news), one technical finding, one practical guide, one product note. Always one CTA (usually: run a snapshot). This keeps Verispect top-of-mind until their trigger event hits — and the trigger (audit/deal/deadline) is when they convert.

## Lifecycle vs nurture (don't confuse)
- **Nurture** = pre-customer, here.
- **Lifecycle/onboarding** = post-signup, in `02-sales/07`.
- A converting lead graduates from nurture → onboarding; remove from nurture to avoid double-emailing.

## Compliance reminders (we of all companies must get this right)
- Consent or legitimate interest documented per contact.
- One-click unsubscribe, honoured immediately + permanently.
- No pixel tracking without disclosure.
- Segment so messages stay role-relevant (irrelevant email erodes legitimate-interest basis).
