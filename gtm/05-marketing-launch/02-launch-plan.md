# Launch Plan

*The spike moments. A solo founder's launch is sequenced, not one big bang — each channel feeds the next.*

---

## Pre-launch (T-4 to T-1 weeks)
- [ ] Landing page live + free snapshot tool working + sample PDF downloadable.
- [ ] Self-serve signup + billing working (or waitlist if not).
- [ ] 3 cornerstone posts published (SEO seeds + credibility).
- [ ] 5–8 design partners onboarded → at least 1–2 quotable testimonials + logos.
- [ ] Build "launch list": everyone who engaged (LinkedIn, snapshot users, waitlist).
- [ ] Assets ready: demo video (2 min), GIFs of drift detection, screenshots, the origin-story post.
- [ ] Warm up: tease build-in-public on LinkedIn for 2–3 weeks ("launching soon, here's what we found").

## Launch sequence (staggered over ~2 weeks)

### Day 1 — LinkedIn founder launch (warmest audience first)
- Origin-story post (the "found bias in an afternoon" narrative) + what Verispect is + CTA "comment 'report' for a free snapshot."
- DM warm connections personally.

### Day 2–3 — Product Hunt
- Launch Tue–Thu. Tagline: "Verispect — active bias & drift detection for production AI." First comment = founder story + the privacy architecture + free snapshot offer.
- Rally design partners + network to support early (genuine, not vote-begging).
- Engage every comment personally all day.

### Day 4 — Show HN / Hacker News
- "Show HN: Verispect – we probe your production LLM for bias/drift, and never see your data." Lead with the technical/privacy angle (HN audience). Be present for hard questions — HN respects honesty about limitations (lean into the methodology-limits doc).

### Day 5–7 — Communities
- Relevant subreddits (r/MachineLearning showcase threads, r/ExperiencedDevs context, RAI/compliance subs — follow each sub's self-promo rules).
- AI Act / Responsible AI / MLOps Slack & Discord communities (value-first, not spam).
- Indie Hackers / founder communities (build-in-public angle).

### Week 2 — Earned & owned
- Pitch the findings piece to AI/compliance newsletters and journalists.
- Guest podcast/newsletter outreach (origin story).
- Email the full launch list with the "we're live + free snapshot" offer.

## Messaging per channel
| Channel | Lead angle |
|---|---|
| LinkedIn | origin story + compliance urgency |
| Product Hunt | product + ease + privacy |
| Hacker News | architecture + honest methodology |
| Reddit/communities | useful findings, not a pitch |
| Newsletters/press | the bias finding + the 2026 deadline |

## Launch-day CTAs (one each)
- Primary: **Run a free snapshot** (one line, 5 min).
- Secondary: book a 15-min demo.
- Tertiary: grab the DPIA template / checklist.

## Post-launch (week 3+)
- Publish a "what we learned launching" post (more reach, build-in-public flywheel).
- Convert snapshot users via the nurture sequence (file 04) + sales sequence C.
- Turn the best launch conversations into design partners / first paid.
- Capture every signup into nurture; nobody falls through.

## Success metrics for launch
- Snapshots run (north-star proxy), signups, demos booked, PH ranking/upvotes, HN front page (bonus), newsletter pickups, launch-attributed MRR over 30 days.

## Risk notes
- Don't launch billing-broken; a waitlist is better than a bad first payment experience.
- HN/Reddit punish hype — bring honesty and code, not marketing speak.
- Respect every community's self-promotion rules (bans hurt a trust brand).
