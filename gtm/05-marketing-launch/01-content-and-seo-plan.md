## SEO mechanics
- One cornerstone per cluster, supporting posts internally linked to it (topic clusters).
- Target long-tail, low-competition compliance terms first (faster to rank, higher intent).
- On-page: clear H1 = keyword, FAQ schema for "what is" queries, fast load, mobile.
- Build authority via: HN/Reddit/LinkedIn distribution, guest posts, getting cited by AI Act newsletters/law blogs (the findings piece is the link magnet).
- Programmatic later: a page per "EU AI Act Article X" and per high-risk domain.

## Distribution (content isn't done when published)
Each piece → LinkedIn post (founder) + relevant communities + newsletter + repurpose into a Twitter/X thread + a short video/Loom for the technical ones. The findings piece → pitch to journalists/newsletters.

## Repurposing engine
1 cornerstone → 1 LinkedIn post + 1 X thread + 3 short posts + 1 newsletter + 1 lead magnet section + demo talking points. Write once, distribute six ways.

## Measurement
Rankings for target terms, organic traffic, time-on-page, magnet conversion per post, assisted demos. Kill/iterate underperformers monthly; double down on what ranks and converts.
