# Verispect Sales Playbook — Overview

*The complete revenue-generation manual. Solo-founder-runnable, GDPR-compliant, honest.*

---

## Sales philosophy

We sell **relief from a specific fear**: "I can't prove my AI behaves, and someone important is about to ask." Not features. Every interaction either (a) makes the fear concrete and urgent, or (b) shows the relief is one line of code away. We never manufacture fear that isn't real, and we never overpromise compliance.

## The two motions

## GDPR-compliant outreach rules (READ THIS — non-negotiable)

Cold B2B email in the EU is governed by GDPR + ePrivacy. To stay clean:
- **Legitimate interest basis**, documented. Only email business addresses of people whose role is plausibly interested (a CTO about AI monitoring = legitimate; spraying generic inboxes = not).
- **Relevance test:** the message must be relevant to their professional role. Ours is, when we target correctly.
- **Identify yourself** clearly (real name, company, address in footer).
- **Easy opt-out** in every email; honour it immediately and permanently (suppression list).
- **No purchased/scraped consumer data.** Build lists from public professional sources (LinkedIn, company sites, Crunchbase) — business contact data, role-relevant.
- **Keep records** of why each contact was deemed legitimate interest (for accountability).
- **Honour PECR/ePrivacy** national variations; some countries (e.g. Germany) are stricter — when in doubt, prefer LinkedIn (relationship-based) over cold email there, or get a soft opt-in.
- **Data minimisation:** store only what outreach needs; set retention; let people be forgotten.

> We are a *compliance* company. Our outbound must be exemplary. A GDPR complaint about our cold email would be brand-fatal. When unsure, don't send — connect on LinkedIn instead.

## Tone & voice in sales

- Peer-to-peer engineer/founder voice, not "enterprise sales rep."
- Short. Specific. One idea per message. Lead with their world, not our product.
- Proof over adjectives. Show the code, the score, the PDF.
- Never use FUD that isn't true. The real situation is scary enough.
