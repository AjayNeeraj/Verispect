# Qualification & Deal Management

*Lightweight MEDDPICC for a solo founder. Don't over-process — but know these 8 things before forecasting a deal as real.*

---

## MEDDPICC-lite

| Letter | Question | What "good" looks like for Verispect |
|---|---|---|
| **M** Metrics | What measurable win? | "Pass enterprise security review," "close X stalled deal," "clear AI Act risk-register line by {date}" |
| **E** Economic buyer | Who controls budget? | Founder/CTO (Pro) or Head of AI / VP Eng with budget (Enterprise) — *named* |
| **D** Decision criteria | How will they choose? | Privacy architecture, report quality, ease of integration, price |
| **D** Decision process | Steps + timeline to signature | Demo → pilot → security/legal → contract; with dates |
| **P** Paper process | Security/DPO/legal review needed? | Identify early; send legal pack proactively |
| **I** Identify pain | The real trigger | Audit / procurement / deadline / incident — concrete, dated |
| **C** Champion | Who sells internally for us? | Often the DPO (after privacy story) or the Head of AI |
| **C** Competition | Incumbent / alternative | Helicone/LangSmith, build-in-house, or do-nothing |

A deal isn't "committed" until M, E, Champion, and Decision process are known.

## Stages & exit criteria (pipeline)

| Stage | Exit criterion | Forecast category |
|---|---|---|
| Lead | ICP-fit confirmed | — |
| Discovery done | Pain + trigger + owner captured | Pipeline |
| Demo done | "Aha" landed + next step booked | Pipeline |
| Pilot active | Real traffic + success criteria agreed in writing | Best case |
| Security/legal | Legal pack in review, no red flags | Commit |
| Verbal | Buyer said yes, paperwork pending | Commit |
| Closed-won | Contract signed / Pro subscription live | Won |

## Pilot management (Enterprise)
- **Always define success criteria up front**, in writing: e.g. "Verispect surfaces ≥1 genuine drift/bias signal AND produces a report the DPO accepts AND integration < 1 day."
- Duration: 2–4 weeks. Longer pilots die.
- Mid-pilot check-in at the halfway point — review early findings, pre-handle objections.
- End-of-pilot: present findings against the agreed criteria → ask for the business.

## Forecasting (solo-founder simple)
Weekly: list deals, stage, € value, next step + date, blocker. If a deal has no next step with a date, it's not real — fix or drop it.

## When to walk away
- No trigger + no timeline + no owner after two touches → nurture list, stop selling.
- Wants fully air-gapped on-prem before SOC 2 → revisit later.
- Price-shopping a logging tool with no compliance pain → wrong ICP.

Time is the only non-renewable resource for a solo founder. Disqualify fast and kindly.

## Handoff to onboarding
On closed-won, trigger `07-onboarding-and-expansion.md` immediately — the first value moment (first report generated) should happen within days, not weeks.
