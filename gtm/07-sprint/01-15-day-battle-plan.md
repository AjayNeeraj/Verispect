# The 15-Day Sales Sprint — Battle Plan

*CEO + management hat. One screen, the whole war. Goal: 10 sales minimum, 20 stretch. Every day has a number.*

---

## The honest funnel math (read this first — CFO being straight)

To close **15** founding customers in 15 days, working backward at realistic solo rates:

| Stage | Conv. rate | Needed |
|---|---|---|
| Closes | — | **15** |
| Qualified demos / setup calls | ~35% close (offer is strong + risk-free) | ~43 demos |
| Free snapshots run | ~35% → demo | ~123 snapshots |
| Positive replies | ~40% → snapshot | ~110 replies |
| Personalized touches sent | ~7% reply | **~1,500 touches** |

→ **~100 personalized touches/day** across LinkedIn + email, **~8 snapshots/day**, **~3 demos/day**, **~1 close/day**.

**Reality check (CEO being honest):** 100 quality touches/day solo is the ceiling and it's brutal. Two levers make 10–20 actually reachable:
1. **The free snapshot does the selling** — it's self-qualifying and self-demoing; many close with light touch, not a full call.
2. **Volume × tight targeting** — every touch lands on a real ICP with a real deadline. Generic spray fails; surgical outreach at this volume works.

If you hit ~60–80 great touches/day, expect **8–12 closes** (still hits the floor). 100+/day with a warm launch spike → **15–20**. The plan below is built to make that volume *possible* by front-loading the list and templating everything.

## Pre-Sprint (Day 0 — must be done before Day 1)
- [ ] Free snapshot tool live + tested (the engine of the whole sprint).
- [ ] Founding-20 campaign page live (`04-campaign-landing-page.md`) with spot counter + Aug 2 countdown.
- [ ] Billing live — at minimum a payment link (Payoneer invoice / Dodo / Paddle) for $15,000/yr and $1,500/mo (a link is enough; full self-serve can wait).
- [ ] Sample report PDF + one-pager + badge mockup ready to send.
- [ ] **Target list: 300+ Tier-1 prospects loaded** (`03-target-list-playbook.md`). This is the day-0 bottleneck — build it first.
- [ ] Sending infra: warmed email domain (SPF/DKIM/DMARC), LinkedIn ready, calendar booking link.
- [ ] Tracker open (`05-sprint-tracker.xlsx`).

> If billing isn't ready, sell anyway: take the verbal yes, send a payment link, set up their snapshot. Don't let infra block a hot lead.

## The daily rhythm (every single day)
**Morning block (outbound, 2–3h):** 50–60 new personalized touches (LinkedIn DMs + emails). Respond to every overnight reply first.
**Midday block (convert, 2–3h):** run booked demos/setup calls; send snapshots to repliers; close.
**Afternoon block (follow-up + content, 1–2h):** follow-up cadences; 1 LinkedIn post; update tracker; book tomorrow.
**End-of-day (15 min):** log numbers in tracker, line up tomorrow's list.

## Week 1 — IGNITE (build pipeline + first closes)

**Day 1 — Launch the campaign.** LinkedIn founding-offer announcement post (origin story + Aug 2 + "Founding 20, comment 'ready'"). Send first 50 DMs to warmest Tier-1. DM every existing connection in ICP. Goal: 30+ snapshots offered, first 5 replies.

**Day 2 — Volume + Product Hunt prep.** 60 touches. Run first snapshots. Book first demos. Email sequence to cold Tier-1 begins.

**Day 3 — First closes.** Run demos, close the easy yeses (warm network + snapshot-flagged accounts). Target: **2 closes.** Post "Founding 20 — 3 spots taken" (social proof + scarcity engine starts).

**Day 4 — Product Hunt / Show HN launch.** Spike day. Lead with privacy + the deadline. Drive everyone to the snapshot → founding offer. Heavy reply management.

**Day 5 — Convert the spike.** Snapshot users from launch → demos → closes. Target cumulative: **5 closes.**

**Day 6 (lighter / weekend) — Follow-up + content.** Chase warm-no-reply with value (sample report, "X of 20 left"). Write a findings post. Partnerships outreach (RAI consultancies, law firms) for referrals.

**Day 7 — Week-1 review.** Tracker review: what message/segment converts? Double down. Target cumulative: **6–7 closes.**

## Week 2 — CLOSE (push to the number)

**Day 8 — Scarcity push.** "Over halfway — X of 20 spots left." Re-touch every warm lead with the spot count. 60 touches into fresh Tier-1 + Tier-2.

**Day 9 — Demo-heavy.** Stack demos. Use closed logos as proof ("companies like {X} just joined"). Target cumulative: **9–10 closes (FLOOR HIT).**

**Day 10 — Referrals + partners.** Ask every customer for 1 intro. Activate any partner referrals. Re-engage nurture leads from the prior GTM build.

**Day 11 — Final-spots urgency.** "Last {N} founding spots before public pricing." Personal re-touch to every hot lead. Push annual.

**Day 12 — Convert the fence-sitters.** Offer the monthly fallback to anyone hesitating on annual. Remove every remaining friction. Target cumulative: **13–15.**

**Day 13 — Content + earned reach.** Pitch findings piece to a newsletter/journalist; "we signed our Founding 20" story angle. Keep closing.

**Day 14 — Last call.** "Founding 20 closes tomorrow / nearly full." Final personal touches to all warm leads. Close verbal yeses with payment links.

**Day 15 — Close + bank + report.** Final closes. Bank the cash. Onboard everyone (white-glove). Publish the win. Tally: **target 15, floor 10, stretch 20.**

## Management cadence (managing yourself)
- **Daily standup (solo, 5 min, morning):** yesterday's numbers, today's #1 priority, biggest blocker.
- **Mid-sprint pivot (Day 7):** if closes < 6, the *offer or targeting* is wrong, not the volume — fix the message before pushing more volume. If replies are high but closes low, the demo/close step leaks — tighten the call.
- **Energy management:** this pace is unsustainable past ~15 days by design. It's a sprint. Protect sleep; a foggy founder closes nothing.

## If we're behind on Day 10 (contingency)
1. Drop friction: lead with **$1,500/mo** (no annual ask) — or offer the one-time $2,900 Readiness Report SKU as the entry.
2. Widen ICP to Tier-2 + UK + broader GCC.
3. Add a one-time **"AI Act Readiness Report" SKU at $2,900** (no subscription commitment) — captures the "just need the document now" buyer; upsell monitoring later.
4. Lean harder on the free snapshot as a no-call self-close.

## If we hit 20 before Day 15 (good problem)
Open "Founding 20 — Wave 2" at a higher locked rate ($1,900/mo), or close founding and move to the public $2,500 with a waitlist. Scarcity stays real.
