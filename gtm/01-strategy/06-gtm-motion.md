# Go-To-Market Motion

*How a lead becomes revenue. The end-to-end engine, owner = founder until first hire.*

---

## Motion summary

**Founder-led, compliance-triggered, product-led-assisted.** Three reinforcing engines:

1. **Outbound** — tight ICP, high-signal cold email + LinkedIn, founder voice. (Primary in months 0–6.)
2. **Inbound/content** — own the "EU AI Act monitoring / LLM bias detection" search and community space. (Compounding; seed early.)
3. **Product-led wedge** — free bias snapshot report + free tier that produces a shareable artifact, driving signups and word-of-mouth.

## The land → expand path

- **Land:** Free snapshot report (lead magnet) → Pro self-serve (€99/mo) for startups; or Enterprise pilot for regulated scale-ups.
- **Expand:** more models monitored, more seats, white-label reports, custom probes, SLA → Enterprise tier. Natural expansion trigger = an upcoming audit or a new enterprise customer of *theirs* demanding evidence.
- **Retain:** the accumulating audit history is the lock-in. Monthly auto-generated report = recurring felt value. Drift alerts = recurring "saved me" moments.

## Sales model by tier

- **Free / Pro:** product-led, self-serve, no human required to buy. Founder intervenes only to convert high-fit Pro users to Enterprise.
- **Enterprise:** founder-led, 3–6 week cycle: discovery → demo → pilot (2–4 wks on real traffic) → security/legal review (send legal pack) → contract. ACV €2k–10k/mo.

## Design-partner program (months 0–3)

Offer 5–8 ideal-fit companies: **free Pro for 6 months + direct founder support + influence on the probe roadmap**, in exchange for: a logo/testimonial, a reference call, and weekly feedback. This buys credibility (logos), product truth, and the case studies that fuel all later marketing.

## Metrics that matter (north-star + guardrails)

- **North star:** # of audit-ready reports generated per month (proxy for delivered value).
- **Revenue:** MRR, net revenue retention, Pro→Enterprise expansion rate.
- **Funnel:** cold reply rate, snapshot→demo, demo→paid, CAC payback.
- **Product:** activation = "integrated SDK + first drift event surfaced," time-to-first-report, weekly active accounts.
- **Trust:** security-review pass rate, DPA requests fulfilled.

## 90-day GTM plan (sequenced)

**Weeks 1–2:** Finish self-serve billing + multi-model + alerts. Stand up landing page (web copy in `05-marketing-launch`). Publish free snapshot tool. Build first 300-account Tier-1 list.

**Weeks 3–6:** Launch outbound (sequences in `02-sales`). Publish 3 cornerstone content pieces (EU AI Act monitoring guide, "we found bias in an afternoon," methodology deep-dive). Recruit design partners.

**Weeks 7–10:** Product Hunt / Show HN / LinkedIn launch. Convert design partners' feedback into v1.1. First Pro conversions. First Enterprise pilot.

**Weeks 11–13:** Publish first case study. Open referral/partner conversations. Tune sequences on real reply data. Review metrics, double down on best channel.

## Guiding constraint
Every motion must respect the operating principles: GDPR-clean outreach, honest compliance claims, no dark patterns. The brand *is* the trust — protect it in every email.
