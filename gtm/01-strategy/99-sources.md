**Key dated facts (as of 2026-05):**
- 2 Aug 2026 remains the operative high-risk (Annex III) compliance date *unless/until* the Digital Omnibus is formally adopted and published in the Official Journal.
- If adopted, standalone Annex III obligations move to **2 Dec 2027**; Annex I embedded AI to **2 Aug 2028**.
- Always state both in customer-facing material. Do not present the delay as certain.
