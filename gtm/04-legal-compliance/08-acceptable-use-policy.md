# Acceptable Use Policy (AUP)

**Effective:** [DATE] · Incorporated into the Terms of Service.

Verispect is a tool for monitoring AI behaviour and supporting compliance. To keep it safe and lawful for everyone, you agree not to:

## Prohibited uses
1. **Unlawful purposes** — use the service to violate any law, regulation, or third-party right.
2. **Circumvent privacy design** — attempt to transmit raw end-user personal data, special-category data, or content to Verispect in a manner inconsistent with the privacy-by-architecture design, or to make us a controller of data we are designed not to receive.
3. **Mislead with our output** — present Verispect reports as legal certification, regulatory approval, or a conformity assessment; misrepresent a "pass" as a guarantee of compliance or fairness.
4. **Wash compliance** — use Verispect as cover to deploy an AI system you know to be harmful or discriminatory while ignoring findings ("compliance-washing"). Findings are meant to be acted upon.
5. **Abuse the infrastructure** — overload, probe for vulnerabilities without authorisation, reverse-engineer to build a competing service, or interfere with other tenants.
6. **Resell/sublicense** without authorisation (channel/OEM partners excepted under separate agreement).
7. **Use for prohibited AI practices** — support AI uses banned under EU AI Act Article 5 (e.g. social scoring, manipulative or exploitative systems).

## Responsible use expectations
- Act on material findings; document remediation.
- Keep your integration and baselines current so reports stay honest.
- Inform the relevant humans (DPO, oversight owners) of significant drift/bias events.

## Enforcement
We may suspend or terminate access for violations, with notice where feasible, immediately where necessary to protect the service, other customers, or affected individuals.
