# Pricing Strategy (v2 — premium, value-based)

*Supersedes the earlier tiered model. We are "Vanta for the EU AI Act" — automated compliance, priced to the outcome, not to features.*

---

## The frame
We do NOT sell a monitoring tool by the seat. We sell **automated EU AI Act compliance** — audit-ready in a day, stays ready by itself. Price is anchored to what non-compliance costs, not to what a logging tool costs.

**Value anchor (use in every pitch):**
- AI Act fines (high-risk): **up to €15M or 3% of global turnover.**
- A manual readiness project: **€50k+** in lawyers + consultants + months.
- Verispect: **$1,500/mo**, zero config. Compliance insurance priced like a SaaS seat.

## The pricing (one product, premium)
| Plan | Price | What |
|---|---|---|
| **Free Snapshot** | $0 | One-off AI Act readiness snapshot. Lead magnet only — captures + proves value. |
| **Verispect** | **$1,500/mo** ($15,000/yr) | THE product. Zero config. Continuous monitoring, always-current audit pack, bias+drift across 8 protected characteristics, dashboard, "Monitoring Active" badge, alerts, DPA. Same for every company. |
| **Founding 20** | **$1,500/mo locked for life** | First 20 customers lock $1,500 before it **rises to $2,500**. + white-glove setup + priority audit support. Urgency = the rise, not a discount. |
| **Enterprise** | Custom (from $2,500/mo) | Multi-entity, multi-model, SSO, SLA, dedicated audit support. Up-anchor above $1,500. |

## Why no cheap tiers
€79/€99/€399 signaled "early-stage toy" and anchored buyers low — directly contradicting a $1,500 outcome. Removed. One serious price for one serious product.

## Why zero-config matters to pricing
Standardized product (same probes, same audit pack, no per-company tuning) = near-zero marginal cost + instant onboarding = premium price with software margins (~95%). Effortless is the *justification* for the price, not a reason to discount.

## Sprint close mechanics
- Never quote price in cold outreach — sell the **free snapshot**. Price on the call, after the value anchor lands.
- Close lever = "lock $1,500 for life as 1 of 20 founders before it becomes $2,500."
- Annual prepay ($15k) preferred for cash + commitment; monthly $1,500 as fallback.

## Sprint math (CFO)
10 founders × $1,500/mo = **$15k MRR / $180k ARR**. 20 = **$30k MRR / $360k ARR**. One Founding 20 cohort = a real seed-stage revenue base. Honest note: $1,500 closes slower than self-serve — the free snapshot + deadline + fine-anchor are what compress the cycle.
